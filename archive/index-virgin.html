The Berlin Sessions

On the 23rd and 24th January 2014, the International Campaign to Abolish Nuclear Weapons (ICAN) will launch a new forum, bringing together international campaigners from the various corners of the humanitarian world. ICAN aims to change the narrative of disarmament campaigning by establishing a unified European network. ICAN is an international campaign-partnership of 331 organisations in 81 countries. Our primary objective is to make states and civil society recognize the catastrophic humanitarian consequences of nuclear weapons, and start a process to make an international treaty to outlaw them. ICAN is not only reframing the issue, but we are also aiming at changing the methods of NGO-campaigning. We do impactful disarmament campaigning in a fresh way by embracing and encouraging entrepreneurs, creative people and big thinkers into our folds.
